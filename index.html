About me

An enthusiastic Software Engineer with 5+ years of experience following agile and waterfall methodologies, implementing a range of technology across the full stack. Thrives in collaborative and creative environments where communication and business user interaction is not only valued, but encouraged. I recently moved from Ireland to Canada and I am looking for a fun, success driven environment where I have the opportunity to grow as a developer and contribute to a great company while also developing real friendships with my colleagues.

Why me

From previous roles as a full stack developer I have found that my passion lies within front end development. This experience has helped me gain an understanding of "the big picture" which in my opinion is invaluable when designing and developing the front end of a product. Along with this a key strength of mine is being a bridging point between developers and non developers when solidifying a vision to create the best product possible. I enjoy working in a fast paced environment where I can contribute and grow as a developer while collaborating with talented colleagues. In my spare time I enjoy listening to tech talks and have developed tools with this knowledge in previous roles to help with processes within the company.
